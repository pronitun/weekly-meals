      Weekly Shopping List

      This list is organized for two shopping trips per week to ensure you have the freshest ingredients. Quantities are based on the 4-portion recipes for your entire week.

      Shopping Trip 1 (for Monday - Wednesday)

      • Proteins:
        • 1 block (227g) paneer
        • 4 salmon fillets
        • 454g shrimp
        • 4 chicken thighs
      • Produce:
        • 1 medium onion
        • 2 paprika
        • 2 zucchini
        • 1 head of broccoli
        • 2 cups mushrooms
        • 1 bag of fresh spinach
        • 1 head of garlic
        • 1 lemon
        • 1 cucumber
        • Fresh dill and mint
        • Fresh parsley
        • 4 large baking potatoes
      • Pantry & Other:
        • Olive oil
        • Garlic powder
        • Salt & pepper
        • 450 ml pasta
        • Red pepper flakes (optional)
        • Parmesan cheese
        • Rice (1 bag)
        • Quinoa (1 bag)
        • Bulgur (1 bag)
        • 1 large container of plain Greek yogurt
        • Dried rosemary

      Shopping Trip 2 (for Thursday - Sunday)

      • Proteins:
        • 1 block extra-firm tofu
        • 2 chicken breasts
      • Produce:
        • 1 medium onion
        • 1 paprika
        • 1 zucchini
        • 1 head of broccoli
        • 1 cup mushrooms
        • Fresh spinach (small bag)
        • Fresh cilantro or parsley
      • Pantry & Other:
        • Soy sauce
        • Ginger
        • Sesame oil
